07 · How I Work

My approach

01
Data Before Decisions
Every engagement opens with a structured kickoff: stakeholder mapping, a signed scope baseline, a risk register, and quantified success metrics. No project progresses without a definition of done. Stakeholders always know what success looks like — in numbers.
02
Discipline Over Heroics
Weekly status cycles with written summaries, a RAG health tracker, and a live issues log shared with all stakeholders. Risks get escalated before they become incidents. Scope changes go through formal change control — every time, no exceptions.
03
Bridge, Don't Translate
Two documents per major milestone: a technical summary for engineering and a business-language decision paper for the executive sponsor. Stakeholders make better decisions when they understand what they're deciding — that's the job.
04
Iterate With Intent
Two-week sprints with written sprint goals, daily async standups, and structured retrospectives. Each release is preceded by UAT sign-off and a go-live readiness checklist. Post-launch hypercare stays active until teams are self-sufficient.
